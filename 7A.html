BACK TO THE TITLE ABOUT THE ANGEL of DEATH.
YES THERE IS AN ANGEL OF DEATH.
AND HIS PURPOSE IS THIS.

WHEN A PERSON ON THE EARTH REACHES THEIR TIME OF DEATH(END OF THEIR LIFE ON EARTH) ,THEN THE ANGEL OF DEATH COMES TO THAT PERSON.
EACH PERSON'S TIME ON EARTH IS DETERMINED,AND THE ANGEL OF DEATH IS SENT WHEN THE  TIME OF EACH PERSONS END COMES.
WHEN THEIR TIME TO DIE REACHES.

THE ANGEL OF DEATH REMOVES THE SPIRIT OF THAT PERSON FROM ITS FLESH(PERMANENT SEPARATION OF THE SPIRIT AND FLESH)
THE FLESH RETURNS TO THE EARTH FROM WHENCE IT CAME(GENESIS 2:7 AND 2:23)

IF AT THAT POINT IN THEIR END ,THE SPIRIT HAS MORE GOOD THAN EVIL(MORE LIGHT THAN DARKNESS)
THEN THE ANGEL OF DEATH HANDS THAT SPIRIT TO THREE ANGELS OF HEAVEN WHO ARE ASSIGNED TO TAKE THAT SPIRIT TO PARADISE
OR EVEN HEAVEN IN SOME RARE CASES.

IF THAT SPIRIT HAS MORE EVIL THAN GOOD(MORE DARKNESS THAN LIGHT),THE ANGEL OF DEATH RIPS THAT SPIRIT FROM THE FLESH AND HANDS IT OVER TO THREE FALLEN ANGELS ASSIGNED TO DRAG THAT SPIRIT TO HADES(HELL)

IF THAT SPIRIT HAS EQUAL MEASURE OF GOOD AND EVIL,THE ANGEL OF DEATH RETURNS THAT SPIRIT TO THE DUST OF THE EARTH.

DUE TO SIMULTANEOUS DEATHS HAPPENNING ON THE EARTH AT A GIVEN TIME,THE ANGEL OF DEATH HAS BEEN GIVEN THE POWER OF OMNIPRESENCE TO ENABLE HIM TO PERFOM HIS WORK AT THE SAME TIME,ANYWHERE ON EARTH.

IN A CASE WHERE THE LORD OR ANOTHER ANGEL IS SENT TO SLAY MULTITUDES ON EARTH,THEY ALSO WILL BE ACCOMPANIED BY THE ANGEL OF DEATH,WHO IN SUCH A CASE WILL BE LIMITED TO PERFOMING THE TASK OF DELIVERING THOSE SPIRITS AFTER THE SLAYING, TO THEIR RESPECTIVE DESTINATIONS.
