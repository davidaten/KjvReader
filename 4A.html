SO WHY THE DIFFERENCE IN NAME?

Back in 2011 ,I asked PRINCE RAPHAEL THAT QUESTION,AND THIS IS THE ANSWER HE GAVE ME.

RAPHAEL TOLD ME THAT.

THE NAMES ARE GIVEN AS SUCH TO SHOW THE ASCENSION IN THE PERSON BEARING THAT SPIRIT.

 

THE DELIVERER 182 PERSON WHILE IN HEAVEN,CAN GO FORTH AS ANY OF THOSE NAMES.

FOR EXAMPLE,IF THE LORD FINDS ONE TO BE WORTHY OF HOSTING THAT SPIRIT,

182 WILL BE FIRST SENT FROM DELIVERER 182 PERSON,

THEN AS THE PERSON ASCENDS,THE 182 PERSON WILL BE SENT,

AND SO ON TILL THE PERSON ON EARTH BECOMES AS THE PERSON IN HEAVEN(DELIVERER 182 PERSON)

 

THE DELIVERER 182 PERSON CAN SPLIT HIMSELF INTO FOUR DIRECTIONS(LIKE THE CROSS) AND GO FORTH

 

WHEN A PERSON IS ON THE EARTH AS EITHER 182 OR 182 PERSON,THEN THEIR GUARDIAN ANGEL IS ALWAYS ANGEL MORPHIET.

BUT WHEN HE ASCENDS TO .THE DELIVERER 182 PERSON,THEN ANGEL GLENN,IS THE ONE WHO IS SENT TO BE HIS GUARDIAN ANGEL.

ANGEL GLENN IS THE ANGEL OF THE DELIVERER 182 IN HEAVEN,AND ALSO ON THE EARTH.

ANGEL GLENN ALSO SITS IN THE COUNSEL OF ANGELS.

THE DELIEVERER 182 PERSON IS A DESCENDANT OF THE HOLYSPIRIT AND THE ANGEL OF THE HOLYSPIRIT IS ANGEL RAPHAEL.

THE PERSON BEARING THE SPIRIT OF  THE DELIVERER 182 PERSON CAN EVEN ASCEND FURTHER TO HOST ANY PERSON OF THE HOLYSPIRIT
