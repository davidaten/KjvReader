HELL ( PART ONE)

THERE ARE MANY PASSAGES FROM EARTH TO HELL.IN FACT EVERY COUNTRY ON EARTH HAS AT LEAST ONE GATEWAY  TO HELL,ALTHOUGH OTHER COUNTRIES HAVE MORE THAN ONE.

 

WHEN ONE IN SPIRIT USES ONE OF THE MANY WAYS TO HELL,THE ROAD TO HELL GETS HOTTER AS ONE DESCENDS,AND ALSO THE CONTENT AND CONCENTRATION OF SULPHUR INCREASES DOWNWARDS,AND LIGHT ALSO DECREASES UNTIL TOTAL DARKNESS IS REACHED(PITCH BLACK)

FROM EARTH TO THE GATE OF HELL IS APPROXIMATELY TEN SECONDS.

AND ALL ROADS FROM EARTH TO HELL CONVERGE AT THE GATE OF HELL.

AND THERE IS NO OTHER ENTRANCE TO HELL EXCEPT BY THAT GATE.

AND IT IS A VERY WIDE AND BIG GATE,AND THE GATE IS BLACK.

IT IS A TWO LEAVED GATE AND URIEL GUARDS THIS GATE.

AND THE OUTLINE /PERIMETER OUTLINE/BORDER LINE  OF THE GATE IS OF FIRE,AND AT THE MIDDLE OF THE GATE IS WRITTEN IN VERY BIG WORDS.

 

WELCOME

      TO

H E L L

YOUR HOME

FOR ETERNITY.

 

MANY PEOPLE WHEN THEY READ THIS,ESPECIALLY THOSE DESTINED FOR HELL,WANT TO GO BACK ,BUT THEY CAN’T,AS THEY ARE ALREADY BEING DRAGGED BY FALLEN ANGELS .

THE FALLEN ANGELS DRAG ONE BY THE LEGS(ONE IS LYING DOWN AND BEING PULLED/DRAGGED ALONG BY THE LEGS)

THE WORDS WRITTEN ON THE GATES OF HELL DOES SHOCK THOSE DESTINED FOR HELL,AND THEY TRY TO IMAGINE THAT THEY ARE IN A VERY HORRIFIC NIGHTMARE AND SHOULD WAKE UP,BUT ITS  NOT A NIGHTMARE BUT RATHER ITS REAL.

THE WORDS ON THE GATE OF HELL ARE ALSO WRITTEN IN FIRE (LAVA)AND AT THE TOP LEFT AND RIGHT CORNER OF THE TWO LEAVED GATES OF HELL,ARE TWO VERY BIG HORNS,ONE ON EACH CORNER.

MUCH LIKE THE HORNS OF A GIGANTIC BULL.

WHEN THE GATES ARE OPENED,

THAT IS WHEN ONE HEARS THE SHOUTS SCREAMS AND CRIES OR THE RESIDENTS OF HELL,AND THE HEAT AND STENCH OF HELL,AND SEES THE FIRE OF HELL.

THOUGH IT APPEARS AS IF THERE IS A ROAD TO GO INSIDE(THINKING THAT YOU ARE STEPPING ON IT),WHEN ONE STEPS  INSIDE,THEY  FALL.

THIS IS THE FIRST LEVEL OF HELL.

AND AS ONE CONTINUES TO WALK THE ROAD,THEY WILL CONTINUE FALLING TO THE SECOND LEVEL,AND SO ON TILL THE NINTH LEVEL.

SO IF ONE IS DESTINED TO THE NINTH LEVEL,THEY MUST FALL NINE TIMES.

 

SO A SPIRIT WILL BE TAKEN TO WHICHEVER LEVEL IT IS DESTINED FOR.

AND ALL THROUGH THE LEVELS,THERE ARE HORRIFIC CREATURES,DEMONS AND FALLEN ANGELS,AND THEY ARE AN EYE SORE AND LAUGH ,MOCK ,GRUNT AND PRODUCE  ALL SORT OF NOISES THAT CANNOT EVEN BE IMAGINED BY A NORMAL  PERSON.

ONE IS NOW IN HELL,AND THAT IS WHERE THE TORTURE STARTS.

EVEN SOME RESIDENTS OF HELL(PEOPLE) ARE HAPPY TO SEE MORE PEOPLE BEING BROUGHT TO HELL.
THEY ARE HAPPY THEY ARE NOW NOT ALLONE,AND HOPE THE FIRE AND SULPHURE WILL REDUCE THE BURNING WITH MORE PEOPLE ADDED.
IN HELL YOU WISH YOU ARE IN A NIGHMARE THAT YOU WANT TO WAKE OF FROM,AS OPPOSED TO HEAVEN WHERE YOU FEEL YOU ARE INA DREAM THAT YOU DONT WANT TO WAKE UP FROM!

FOR IN THEIR MINDS THEY BELIEVE THAT THE MORE PEOPLE THAT ARE BROUGHT IN,IT WILL REDUCE THEIR PAIN AND SUFFERING.

THEY FEEL LIKE THEY WILL BE AT LEAST DISTRACTED AND THE DEMONS AND FALLEN ANGELS WILL WANT TO CONCENTRATE FIRST ON THE NEW ARRIVALS.

SO THEY HOPE AND ARE HAPPY FOR NEW PEOPLE BEING CAST TO HELL.HOWEVER THIS DOES NOT REDUCE THEIR PAIN.

IT CAN BE MUCH COMPARED TO ADDING FUEL TO THE FIRE.

HOWEVER THERE ARE SOME  RESIDENTS IN HELL WHO WILL TELL YOU”I WISH YOU DID NOT HAVE TO COME HERE”

FOR THEY KNOW WHAT THEY HAVE GONE THROUGH SINCE THEY WERE CAST TO HELL AND ARE MERCIFUL ON THEM WHO ARE JUST ENTERING HELL.

FOR THEY KNOW IT IS CONTINUOUS BURNING AND TORTURE AND TORMENT WITHOUT DEATH,FOR ONE CANNOT DIE IN HELL REGARDLESS OF THE FIRE OR TORTURE.

FOR EXAMPLE IN THE FOURTH LEVEL.

SOME IN THIS LEVEL ARE PUT IN A SORT OF COFFIN,THE COFFIN IS UPRIGHT ,BUT THE COFFIN IS HALF THE SIZE OF THE ONE PUT INSIDE.AND THE COFFIN IS METALLIC AND VERY HOT(AS HOT AS FIRE),AND KEEP IN MIND FIRE IN HELL IS HOTTER THAN ON EARTH,IT GOES BEYOND A THOUSAND OF DEGREES.

SO ONE IS SQUEEZED TO FIT INSIDE THE VERY HOT COFFIN,AND THEIR IS ABSOLUTELY NO MOVEMENT POSSIBLE.

THE COFFIN IS COMPLETELY COVERED,ONLY A SMALL OPENING ON THE EYE LEVEL,TO ENABLE THE ONE LOCKED IN THERE TO SEE OUTSIDE.

TO SEE HORRIFIC CREATURES AND DEMONS AND TORMENTS OF HELL.

AND THEY BURN WHILE INSIDE THAT COFFIN AND CANNOT MOVE,FOR THEIR IS NO SPACE TO MOVE,AND CAN ONLY CREAM AND SHOUT.

AND THEY GET TIRED,AND CANNOT SIT BUT MUST STAND BURNING FOR ETERNITY WITHOUT REST OR MOVEMENT!

EVERY LEVEL IN HELL HAS A LAKE OF FIRE.

BUT AT THE NINTH LEVEL IS WHERE THERE IS  THE FATHER OF ALL LAKES OF FIRE.AND HERE IS THE SOURCE OF THE LAKE OF FIRES AND ITS EVEN HOTTER THAN ALL THE OTHER LAKES OF FIRE ON OTHER LEVELS.

ON THE LAKES OF FIRE,IS ONE PLACE OF TORTURE,THAT THE TORMENTORS LIKE TO PUT ONE THEN REMOVE THEM AND RETURN THEM TO THEIR PLACE OF NO REST.
