HELL (PART TWO)

QUESTIONS MEMBERS OF THIS FORUM ASKED,SOME WERE ASKED IN PUBLIC,SOME IN PRIVATE.

QUESTION: There is a lady by the name of Mary K Baxter who wrote the book Divine Revelation of Hell which says that hell is in the shape of a person lying on their back .
In the FOUR BOOKS IT is written that Hell has nine descending levels my question is how are both these things true or how do they relate

ANSWER

WHEN A PERSON IS TAKEN TO HELL,LIKE THAT LADY,THERE IS A LEVEL SHE WAS TAKEN TO,AND IN THAT LEVEL SHE PERCEIVED HELL LOOKING LIKE A PERSON LAYING ON THEIR BACK.

BUT HELL IS IN LEVELS.

HELL IS A MIMIC OF HEAVEN AND IS AN UP SIDE DOWN PYRAMID.

WHEREAS IN HEAVEN WHEREVER LEVEL YOU ARE IN IS SO WONDERFUL,YOU DON’T EVEN HAVE A DESIRE TO BE IN ANOTHER LEVEL.FOR YOU ALREADY ARE SO HAPPY,AND YOUR DESIRES FULFILLED BEYOND YOUR EXPECTATION.

IN HELL,YOU DON’T WANT TO BE TAKEN DOWN,FOR YOU ARE ALREADY SUFFERING BEYOND YOUR WILDEST IMAGINATIONS,YOU CANT EVEN BELIEVE THAT THERE CAN BE WORSE PUNISHMENT BELOW.

SOME DEMONS CAN KEEP ON PLUCKING YOUR  EYES OUT,AND THIS IS VERY PAINFUL,BUT THE EYES WILL RETURN BACK,AND ITS ALSO PAINFUL AS THE EYES RETURN,AND THEY CAN CONTINUE PLUCKING THEM OUT INDEFINITELY.

SO HELL IS NOT ABOUT THE HEART /LEG ETC.

HELL IS LEVELS GOING DOWNWARDS.

MARY K BAXTER IS OF THE BLESSED,AND COULD NOT BE SHOWN THE FULL PICTURE OF  HELL IN  A MUCH DESCRIPTIVE WAY AS IT IS BEING EXPLAINED TO YOU NOW.

 

QUESTION:IS IT POSSIBLE TO EVER GET OUT OF HELL?

ANSWER.

IT IS IMPOSSIBLE FOR ONE TO GET OUT OF HELL BY THEMSELVES.ONLY BY JESUS CHRIST WHEN HE VISITS HELL,AND WHEN ONE IS AMONG THE CHOSEN ONES TO BE REMOVED FROM HELL.

WHEN ONE IS IN SUCH A CASE REMOVED BY JESUS CHRIST  FROM HELL,THAT PERSON WILL BE PUT IN A NEW BORN CHILD ON EARTH,AND GIVEN ANOTHER CHANCE TO SERVE GOD.TO SEE IF THEY WILL THIS TIME ROUND MAKE IT.

BUT THERE IS NO SINGLE CASE OF A MAN WHO WAS BEEN CAST TO HELL ESCAPING.

HOWEVER FOR DEMONS AND FALLEN ANGELS,THEY DO SOMETIMES ESCAPE.TO ASCEND TO EARTH TO GET SOME FRESH AIR,FOR THE AIR IN HELL IS FULL OF SULFUR AND IS ALSO VERY THIN(ONE CAN’T BREATH PROPERLY)

BUT  A MAN WEATHER ONCE BLESSED OR GENTILE OR THE CAANANITE ,ONCE CAST IN HELL,ITS IMPOSSIBLE TO ESCAPE.

 

QUESTION: why is the punishment eternal when the sin committed is in the temporary carnal life?

ANSWER.

ITS ETERNAL BECAUSE ITS ABOUT YOU MAKING A CHOICE.

WHEN A PERSON REACHES THE AGE OF MAKING DECISIONS FOR THEMSELVES,WHEN ONE KNOWS GOOD AND EVIL,ITS UPON THAT PERSON TO MAKE THE RIGHT CHOICE.AND FOLLOW THE FOOTSTEPS OF JESUS CHRIST.BUT IF THEY DON’T AND DO EVIL ,THEN WOE TO THEM.

BECAUSE THEY HAD A CHANCE ON EARTH.JUST LIKE WHEN A LITTLE CHILD DOES WRONG,ITS UPON THE PARENT OF THE CHILD TO KNOW THAT THAT IS NOT RIGHT,AND TO CORRECT IT BEFORE THAT CHILD GROWS UP.

WHEN HE/SHE IS A CHILD THEIR SINS ARE PLACED UPON THEIR PARENTS.AND IF THE PARENTS DON’T CORRECT THOSE SINS,THEN IT MEANS THE PARENTS ARE NOT LEADING THE CHILDREN IN THE WAYS OF GOD,BUT IF THEY CORRECT THE SINS,THEN IT IS BECAUSE THEY ARE LEADING THOSE CHILDREN IN THE WAYS OF GOD.

SO THAT CHILD WILL GROW UP KNOWING AND FEARING GOD,FOR WHO HE IS.

SO WHEN THEY GROW UP AND CHOOSE TO DO EVIL,AND THEY FEEL COMFORTABLE IN THAT EVIL,AND THEY CONTINUE IN THAT EVIL.

EVEN IF THEY CONTINUE IN THE EVIL FOR TWO MONTHS AND THEY DIE IN THAT TIME,THEY WILL NOT GET A CHANCE OF GOING TO HEAVEN,FOR THEY HAD TIME TO MAKE THE RIGHT DECISIONS.

 

QUESTION:Is the devil punished in hell too or is he the punisher?

ANSWER

THE DEVIL WILL PUNISH AND HE DOES PUNISH FOR HE REJOICES TO PUNISH ALL THAT HAVE BEEN CAST TO HELL.

BUT ALSO REMEMBER HELL WAS MADE FOR HIM.AND THAT IS WHY HE WOULD RATHER BE ON EARTH THAN IN HELL.

BECAUSE IN HELL HE ALSO GETS PUNISHED , HE ALSO BURNS.

AND HE FEELS TORMENTED FOR HE DID NOT ACCOMPLISH TAKING OVER HEAVEN.HE WAS NOT ABLE TO ACCOMPLISH HIS DESIRE OF TAKING HEAVEN,AND THE THOUGHT OF THIS ,TORMENTS HIM.

AND THEREFORE HE GETS PUNISHED FOR HELL WAS MADE FOR HIM AND HIS FALLEN ANGELS.

THE DEVIL DOESN’T LIKE BEING IN HELL.

HOWEVER WHILE IN HELL HIS POWER IS UNLIMITED.HE IS THE KING IN HELL,THOUGH HE STILL BURNS.

BUT HE HAS POWER over ANYONE ELSE IN HELL,AND HE IS THE ONE THAT ISSUES ORDERS IN HELL AND HE CAN TORMENT WHOSOEVER HE WISHES   IN HELL.

HOWEVER THERE COMES A TIME WHEN THE DEVIL CAN ALSO BE TORMENTED BY OTHER FALLEN ANGELS AND DEMONS.

THIS USUALLY HAPPENS WHEN THE DEVIL WHILE ON EARTH IS CAST BACK TO HELL FOR DOING THAT WHICH HE WAS NOT AUTHORIZED BY GOD TO DO.

EG ATTEMPTING TO KILL SOMEONE WHO HE HAS NOT BEEN AUTHORIZED BY GOD.

AT SUCH A TIME,THE ANGELS OF GOD TAKE THE DEVIL TO HELL AND CHAIN /TIE HIM THERE AND HE WILL BE TORMENTED BY THE FALLEN ANGELS AND DEMONS FOR AS LONG AS THE LORD WILL ALLOW.

IT CAN BE A MONTH A YEAR ,ACCORDING TO THE SEVERITY OF HIS SINS.

THE FALLEN ANGELS AND DEMONS AT SUCH A TIME,TAKE ADVANTAGE AND TORMENT HIM,IN THE WORST OF WAYS,FOR THEY KNOW IT’S A GOLDEN OPPORTUNITY  TO TORMENT THE DEVIL,WHO RULES WITH AN IRON FIST IN HELL.

THEY WILL MOCK,LAUGH ,PIERCE AND DO ALL SORTS OF HORRIFIC THINGS TO HIM.

BUT REST ASSURED WHEN HE FINALLY IS RELEASED HE TAKES REVENGE ON THEM TO SHOW THEM WHO IS KING,AND ALSO TO INSTILL FEAR IN THEM THAT IN FUTURE THEY SHOULD NEVER TORMENT HIM IF HE EVER IS CAST IN HELL.

BUT NON IN HELL EVER LEARNS,AND WHENEVER ANOTHER IS GIVEN AN OPPORTUNITY TO TORTURE THE OTHER ,THEY GLADLY DO IT.

HOWEVER,WHEN THE FALLEN ANGELS OR DEMONS ARE TORMENTING THE DEVIL,OR THE DEVIL IS IN TURN TORMENTING THEM,THEY FEEL RELIEF FROM THEIR PAINS,AND THAT IS WHY THEY WILL DO IT.

AND FURTHERMORE THEY HATE ONE ANOTHER,AND WILL HAVE  NO MERCY ON ONE ANOTHER.

AND ESPECIALLY WHEN THE DEVIL IS CHAINED IN HELL,HE IS EVEN MORE TORMENTED BY KNOWING THAT HE IS NOT ABLE TO BE ON EARTH AND CAUSE PEOPLE TO DESCEND TO HELL.

FOR HIS MAIN MOTIVE AND PURPOSE IS TO TRY TO PROVE TO GOD THAT HE CAN TAKE DOWN MORE PEOPLE  TO HELL THAN THEY CAN ASCEND TO HEAVEN.

SO THIS LIMITATION IN BEING IN HELL TORMENTS HIM MORE,BY HIS THINKING,MEN ON EARTH ARE ESCAPING HIS GRASP.AND HE DOES NOT BELIEVE THAT ANY FALLEN ANGEL CAN CAUSE AS MANY PEOPLE TO DESCEND TO HELL THAN HIMSELF.

HE FEELS THAT HIS FALLEN ANGELS AND DEMONS ARE NOT WORKING AS HARD AS HE WANTS THEM TO WORK IN BRINGING PEOPLE TO HELL,SO HE IS EVEN MORE TORMENTED.

THERE IS GOOD AND EVIL.

THE LORD IS TRYING TO GET PEOPLE TO HEAVEN BUT LETS THEM DECIDE BY THEMSELVES.

THE DEVIL ON THE OTHER HAND WORKS EVEN HARDER TO TRY AND DRAG PEOPLE TO HELL.

MAINLY TO PROVE TO GOD THAT HE CAN PULL MORE PEOPLE TO HELL THAN GOD CAN TAKE THEM TO HEAVEN.MORE PEOPLE THAN HE INITIALLY FELL FROM HEAVEN WITH.

AND THAT IS ONE REASON THE LORD WILL NEVER FORCE A PERSON ON EARTH TO SERVE HIM.

YOU MUST CHOSE BETWEEN GOD AND THE DEVIL.

ITS UPON YOU TO SERVE AND LOVE GOD WILLINGLY,AND NOT DO EVIL AND THE DEVIL’S WORKS.

 

SO ONE MIGHT ASK,WHY WOULD THE DEVIL RETURN TO HELL WHERE HE WILL BURN RATHER THAN STAYING ON EARTH WHERE HE IS NOT BURNING?

HELL IS THE DEVIL’S KINGDOM,AND THOUGH HE ALSO RULES ON EARTH,EARTH IS NOT HIS KINGDOM.

HERE ON EARTH THE DEVIL CANNOT CONTROL EVERYTHING,BUT IN HELL HE CAN CONTROL ALMOST EVERYTHING.

ON THE EARTH THE DEVIL IS LIMITED IN WHAT HE CAN DO,BUT IN HELL HE IS NOT LIMITED.HE WILL DO ANYTHING TO MAKE HIMSELF FEEL BETTER.BUT STILL HE IS NOT FEELING BETTER FOR HE IS BURNING AND FEELING PAIN THAT HE DID NOT TAKE OVER HEAVEN,AND THAT HE IS NOT MORE POWERFUL THAN GOD.

BUT THAT IS HIS KINGDOM ,AND  HE WILL DO ANYTHING AND ALSO WANTS ALL IN HELL TO KNOW THAT IT IS HIS KINGDOM AND HE IS IN CHARGE,AND THAT MAKES HIM FEEL SOMEWHAT BETTER.

BUT SINCE HE IS MAINLY BASED AT THE NINTH LEVEL,THE FIRE THERE IS HOTTER THAN OTHER LEVELS SO HE BURNS EVEN MORE THAN THE OTHERS.BUT THAT IS WHERE POWER IS AND HE MUST STAY THERE WHERE THERE IS POWER.

SO THAT IS WHY HE AND HIS FALLEN ONES WILL TORTURE OTHERS BEYOND IMAGINATION IN AN ATTEMPT TO TRANSFER THE PAIN THEY ARE FEELING TO OTHERS.

 

QUESTION:Are there other ways to blaspheme THE HOLYSPIRIT ,that we need to be aware of?

ANSWER.

BLASPHEMING THE HOLY SPIRIT CAN BE EASY AND ALSO NOT EASY.

FOR EXAMPLE WHEN JESUS WALKED THE EARTH,THOSE THAT ABUSED HIM AND MOCKED HIM,WERE BLASPHEMING THE HOLY SPIRIT.SINCE THE HOLYSPIRIT WAS WITH HIM.AND JESUS COMES FORTH FROM HIM.YOU CANNOT THINK THAT YOU ARE ABUSING THE SON AND NOT THE FATHER,FOR THE FATHER DOES NOT SEE ANY DIFFERENCE ,THE SON AND THE FATHER ARE ONE.

BUT WHEN ONE FEARS THE LORD,IT IS VERY HARD TO BLASPHEME THE HOLYSPIRIT,FOR YOU KNOW YOUR LIMITS.

FOR THOSE WHO LIKE TO USE THE NAME OF GOD IN VAIN,AND CAN EVEN SWEAR USING THE NAME OF GOD,WHILE KNOWING DEEP DOWN,WHAT THEY ARE SWEARING IS NOT TRUE.

GOD IS TRUTH,SO IF ONE DOES THAT,THEY ARE BLASPHEMING THE HOLYSPIRIT.

QUESTION.

WHAT ABOUT THOSE PEOPLE WHO SPEAK EVIL ABOUT THE BOOKS,OR  ABOUT THE AUTHOR,OR BURN THE BOOKS OR BURN THE HOLY BIBLE ,ARE THEY BLASPHEMING?

ANSWER.

THOSE PEOPLE EVEN DO WORSE,FOR NOT ONLY ARE THEY BLASPHEMING,THEY ALSO DISRESPECT GOD AND HAVE NO FEAR OF GOD.THOSE ARE THE THREE THINGS THEY DO.

ANYTIME YOU WILL TAKE THE HOLY BIBLE AND SWEAR ON IT,YET YOU ARE LYING,YOU ARE BLASPHEMING THE HOLYSPIRIT.

ANYTIME YOU TAKE THE BIBLE OR THE BOOKS AND BURN THEM,OR SPEAK ILL OF THE AUTHOR,YOU ARE STILL BLASPHEMING THE HOLY SPIRIT.

AND WHEN ONE BLASPHEMES,THERE IS NO CHANCE FOR HEAVEN FOR THIS IS AN UNFORGIVABLE SIN.

YOU CANNOT GO BACK AND SAY I AM SORRY.FOR IT WILL NOT BE FORGIVEN.

YOU CANNOT JUST TAKE THE HOLY BIBLE AND BURN IT,ITS LIKE SAYING TO HELL WITH JESUS CHRIST,AND BURNING JESUS CHRIST.

YOU CANNOT  SPEAK EVIL ABOUT THE AUTHOR,AND YOU DON’T EVEN KNOW THE RELATIONSHIP HE HAS WITH GOD.THIS IS BLASPHEMY.YOU DON’T KNOW WHERE HE HAS REACHED WITH GOD,YOU DON’T KNOW WHERE HE STARTED OF WITH GOD.THEN YOU JUST GO ON THE INTERNET OR WHEREVER AND JUST WHILE CONVERSING WITH A FRIEND YOU SAY BAD THINGS ABOUT HIM,YET HE HAS NOT DONE ANYTHING WRONG TO YOU,THIS IS BLASPHEMY  AND WILL NOT BE FORGIVEN.

WHEN YOU SEE THOSE PICTURES,THEY ARE OF HELL AND QUITE ACCURATE.

MANY ARTISTS ARE NOT OF GOD.DEMONS WILL USUALLY POSSESS THOSE ARTISTS AND DRAW PICTURES OF HELL.ITS A WAY OF BRINGING HELL TO THE EARTH.YOU MAY THINK THE ARTISTS ARE VERY CREATIVE AND ARE USING THEIR IMAGINATION,WHILE THE TRUTH IS THAT THERE IS A DEMON IN THEM DRAWING HELL.SO BE VERY CAREFUL,EVEN WHAT YOU WATCH  MOVIES AND TELEVISION.YOU MAY JUST BE WATCHING HELL THROUGH THE EYES OF DEMONS.

HELL HAS FACTORIES THAT ARE IN CONSTANT PRODUCTION OF MATERIALS THAT ARE DESTINED FOR EARTH,AND WILL USE EVERY POSSIBLE WAY TO BRING HELL ON EARTH FROM THEIR VIEWPOINT.

ITS EVEN BETTER TO WATCH MOVIES ABOUT ALIENS.FOR ALIENS EXIST AND ARE REAL,MANY HAVE VERY BIG EYES.ALIENS DON’T KNOW EVIL BUT ARE VERY INTELLIGENT.THEY LIKE DISCOVERING AND ARE VERY CURIOUS.AND ARE VERY ADVANCED.THAT IS WHY THEY CAN VISIT EARTH.

QUESTION.

HAVE ALIENS EVER KIDNAPPED A PERSON FROM EARTH TO GO AND DO EXPERIMENT ON THEM.

ANSWER.

ALIENS HAVE VISITED EARTH MANY TIMES,AND TAKEN SAMPLES BUT THEY CANNOT TAKE A PERSON FROM EARTH,WITH THEM,FOR THE PERSON OF EARTH WILL NOT SURVIVE THE VERY FAST FLIGHT.

THEIR DEPICTION ON MOVIES IS QUITE ACCURATE,HOWEVER THEY ARE ALSO MUCH BIGGER,IN THEIR PRIME AGE UP TO ABOUT 30 FEET,BUT AS THEY GROW OLDER THEY SHRINK,AND SHRINK UNTIL THEY BECOME VERY SMALL AND DISAPPEAR.

SOME PEOPLE ON EARTH HAVE ACTUALLY SEEN THEM WHEN THEY HAPPEN TO LAND ON A PLACE ON EARTH WHERE THE PEOPLE ARE.
