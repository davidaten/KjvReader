MESSAGE FROM THE LORD.

THE FIRST DAY OF OCTOBER,THE DAY OF TRUMPETS,IS A GREAT DAY WHEN THE TRUMPETS HAVE BEEN SOUNDED ON EARTH BY THE FOUR GREAT PRINCES.

THIS IS A HISTORIC MOMENT IN THIS CYCLE.

THE TRUMPETS HAVE BEEN SOUNDED FOR THE OCCUPANTS OF THE EARTH TO KNOW THAT IT HAS COME A TIME WHEN THE GOOD/WORTHY SHALL SEE GOD'S BLESSINGS(WATER) AND THE WICKED OR EVIL WILL SEE GODS WRATH(PLAGUES AND FIRE)

THE LORD HAS PUT A DIVIDING LINE OR DEMARCATION ,AS HE DID IT IN EGYPT,WHERE THE EGYPTIANS IN EGYPT WERE SEPARATED FROM THE HEBREWS IN GOSHEN.

THE LORD'S PATIENCE HAS FADED AWAY,TO THOSE WHO ARE STUBBORN TO HIS INSTRUCTIONS,AND DON'T WANT TO SERVE HIM,AND ON THE OTHER HAND HIS LOVE,IS GETTING IMMEASURABLE TO THOSE WHO ARE SERVING HIM WHOLEHEARTEDLY AND HEARKENING TO HIS INSTRUCTIONS AND COMMANDMENTS.

THIS MONTH OF OCTOBER AND CONTINUING,WE WILL SEE OUR LORD MOVE TO ACTION,FOR HIS TIME OF BEING COVERT HAS COME TO AN END.
