I AM THE FIRST AND I AM THE LAST,AND BESIDE ME THERE IS NO OTHER. I AM HE.

I GIVE LIFE AND I TAKE,HE WHO DISOBEYS AND DOES NOT HEARKEN TO THE WORDS,CASTS THEMSELVES INTO THE FIRE OF HELL.
MY WORDS WILL NEVER GO IN VAIN,BECAUSE I AM HE.

A MILLION YEARS IS LIKE A DAY HAS NOT STARTED.

I PLANTED A MUSTARD SEED,AND IT WILL COME TO PASS,MORE FRUITS WILL COME OUT OF IT.
I AM HE.

IF MAN WILL NOT SERVE ME, I WILL MAKE TREES AND STONES TO SERVE ME.

RAIN OF FIRE WILL RAIN ON THOSE WHOM I GAVE TIME TO REDEEM THEMSELVES,BUT THEY DID NOT HEARKEN TO MY VOICE.

RAIN OF WATER WILL RAIN TO THOSE WHO HEARKEN TO MY WORD AND DO ACCORDING TO THEIR PURPOSES.

IT WILL RAIN TO THEM WHO SERVE ME,WITH THE WHOLE OF THEIR HEARTS,MINDS ,BODY AND SOUL.
THEY WILL NEVER LACK.
THOSE WHO TRUST IN ME WILL FIND PEACE IN THEIR LIVES AND HEARTS.

THOSE WHO HAVE LESS TRUST IN ME WILL FIND HELL IN THEIR HOMES.

SERVE ME WITH LOVE,HONESTY,PATIENCE,COURAGE,
VICTORY,PEACE AND HARMONY.
AND YOU WILL FIND ME IN ALL THOSE, I AM HE.
I WILL GIVE PEACE AND LOVE TO THOSE WHO ASK OF ME.

WHENEVER ONE IS DOWN, ASK AND THE HAND SHALL BE GIVEN UNTO YOU TO HANG ON TO.

WHEN YOU ARE WEAK ASK,AND STRENGTH SHALL BE GIVEN UNTO YOU.

WHEN YOU ARE SICK ASK,AND HEALING SHALL BE GRANTED UNTO YOU.

I AM HE WHO CREATED HEAVEN ,EARTH AND HELL.
BESIDE ME THERE IS NO OTHER.

IN A BLINK OF AN EYE,I CAN GIVE HEAVEN,IN A BLINK OF AN EYE,I CAN GIVE HELL.

SERVE ME AND I WILL SHOW YOU HEAVEN,DISOBEY ME AND I WILL GIVE YOU HELL;WHERE THERE IS NO LIFE,NO PEACE,NO MERCY,NO FORGIVENESS AND A LOT OF TORTURE THROUGHOUT ETERNITY.

HEARKEN TO MY WORDS,YOU WILL FIND PEACE,LOVE,GRACE,ABUNDANCE,
PATIENCE,UNITY AND A LOT OF HEAVENLY THINGS.

MOUNTAINS I CREATED,OCEANS AND THE SEA.
WITHIN A WORD I CAN REMOVE THEM OUT OF YOUR WAY,ONLY IF YOU SERVE ME AND LOVE ME LIKE I DO.

MY CHILDREN, DON'T EVER GIVE UP, THE BATTLE IS LONG.

REMEMBER THE VOWS THAT YOU MADE TO SERVE ME WITH THE WHOLE OF YOUR HEARTS,AND NOTHING WILL EVER COME BETWEEN YOU AND ME,AND YOUR PURPOSE.

STAY AWAY FROM EVIL, BE ON YOUR GUARD .

LISTEN TO YOUR INNER SELVES.LET NO EVIL,LET NO MIND CONTROL YOUR LIFE,LEST YOU SEE THE FIRE OF HELL.

I AM WITH YOU ALL THE TIME, BE STRONG FOR THE TIMES AHEAD.

I PROMISED GOOD AND YOU SHALL SEE IT.

I PROMISED HAPPINESS AND YOU SHALL BE HAPPY.

I PROMISED LOVE AND YOU SHALL FEEL IT.

DON'T LET TOUGH TIMES SNATCH ALL THAT IS IN STORED FOR ALL OF YOU.

LOVE YOU ALL.HOPE YOU WILL HEARKEN TO THE WORDS AND DO ACCORDING TO WHAT YOU ARE EXPECTED.

GOD IS TIMELESS AND NO MATTER HOW LONG YOU MAY FEEL TIME HAS GONE.
I AM STILL HE WHO GIVES LIFE AND TAKES.
